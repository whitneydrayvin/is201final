All You Need to Know About Car Modifications

Have you ever wanted to take you car from bone stock, base model, to a head turning machine?

Well it's really not that hard! It only takes a few hundred thousand dollars, hours of labor, and an undying desire to make your day to day activities way less convenient.


Here is how you get it done:


  1. Collect funds
    • Start a DIY company with the focus of bodywork. This is best accomplished by aquiring a hammer, bondo, and as many flat head screwdrivers as possible. This will give the you the necessary skills to start your own project



    • Short sell all of your friends and family members. Think of anyone you know between the ages of 14-18; this group is especially susceptible to the enticings of a modified car. They are also young, which means that they don't really care what the end product looks like.
    • Lastly, make sure that with every modification you do, add your Instagram handle to each piece. This will insure the growth of your company.


  2. Find time
    • As I said, this process will take many more hours than you could possibly have to spend. It will also put a strain on every aspect of your life. But it's all worth it.



    • Cut out un-necassary things like a third meal, time with your wife and kids, that pesky Saturday shift, even the golf trip with your grandpa.
    • Eventually your heart will soothe and you will be able to find the joy in your car, rather than the things you used to hold most dear :)


  3. Live with inconvenience
    • Prepare yourself to hate every single task that brings you out of the house. Every bump in the road will become your biggest fear. Even a small pebble could mean the difference between having a leaking oilpan on the way home or not.



    • So you thought you could get off your driveway today? Not if it's on a hill.
    • Maybe you will take a quick trip to the mall? Not if theres a parking lot involved.
